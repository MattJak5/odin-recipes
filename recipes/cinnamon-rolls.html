EASY CINNAMON ROLLS!

Homepage

Description

A recipe to make 6 soft cinnamon rolls topped with a sweet cream cheese frosting.

These should take around 3 hours to make from start to finish

Ingredients

Cinnamon rolls

  • 60ml warm water
  • 3g yeast
  • 60ml whole milk
  • 30g room temp unsalted butter
  • 35g caster or granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 lrage egg
  • 245g plain flour plus extra for dusting

Cinnamon sugar filling

  • 30g softened butter
  • 45g brown sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon

Cream cheese frosting

  • 15g softened butter
  • 150g full fat cream cheese, room temp
  • 60g icing sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Add warm water to a bowl and add yeast and let it bloom
  2. Add milk and butter to a heatproof bowl. Heat in the microwave until the butter is melted and the milk is warm, stirring every 15-20 seconds.
  3. In a large bowl add sugar, salt and egg. Then add milk mixture and stir with a wooden spoon or spatula to combine.
  4. Add flour and yeast mixture and mix until it comes together to forma soft dough - It will be sticky
  5. Lightly slour work bench, turn dough out and knead until a soft smooth dough forms.
  6. Form the dough into a ball and place in a lightly greased medium sized bowl. cover with clingfilm and leave to rise for 1 hour or until it has doubled in size.
  7. Turn out dough and roll out into a rectangle roughly 13cm x 30 cm.
  8. Gently smear softened butter all over the dough, to the edges. Combine sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle all over hte butter.
  9. Tightly roll up the dough from the short end, then cut into 6 rolls using a serrated knife. Place the rolls into a greaseprrof paper lined 20cm cake tin. Cover with clingfilm and rest for at least 30 minutes.
  10. Preheat oven to 180ÂºC. Bake rolls for 25 minutes or until golden brown. Meanwhile, make the cream cheese frosting.
  11. Add butter and cream cheese to a large mixing bowl and beat with and electric whisk on medium speed until smooth and creamy. Add sugar and vanilla and mix until smooth.
  12. Smear two thirds of hte frosting on the rolls while still warm. Then add the remaining frosting on each bun when ready to serve.

Best enjoyed the day of making
